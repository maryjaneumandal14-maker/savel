The Hidden Crisis in Research Writing

What does “a hidden crisis in research writing” mean to you, and how might inadequate referencing contribute to that crisis?

Answer:A hidden crisis in research writing” means that many students and researchers struggle with producing accurate and credible research, but the problem is often unnoticed.It referencing contributes to this crisis because it makes it hard to verify information, lowers the credibility of the work.

Why might fake or AI-generated references be harmful to the academic community and to your own research career?

Answer:Fake or AI-generated references are harmful because they mislead readers, undermine trust in academic work, and can seriously damage a researcher’s reputation.

Reflection: This made me realize that proper referencing is essential not only for honesty but also for building trust in the academic community. Using accurate sources helps me develop credibility and become a responsible researcher.And always remember that Ai is not always right.
